Our Mission

Welcome to our Disease Prediction web application! While searching for symptoms online may lead to unreliable and varying results, our platform utilizes advanced machine learning techniques to provide accurate disease predictions based on the symptoms you report. We employ models such as Support Vector Machine (SVM), Naive Bayes, and Random Forest to deliver reliable insights. Our user-friendly interface allows you to easily input symptoms and receive potential diagnoses along with treatment options for various conditions. We prioritize your health and aim to empower you with precise information, guiding you toward appropriate medical care. Explore our features, learn about common diseases, and take the first step towards understanding your health better with confidence!
